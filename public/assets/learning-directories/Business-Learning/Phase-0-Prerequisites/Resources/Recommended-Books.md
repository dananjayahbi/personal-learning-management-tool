## How to Read Effectively

### Active Reading Tips:
1. **Take Notes**: Write down key insights
2. **Apply Immediately**: Use concepts in CoodLoom
3. **Discuss**: Talk through ideas with co-founder
4. **Reread Key Sections**: Go back to important parts
5. **Create Action Items**: Every book should lead to action

### Reading Schedule:
- **Daily**: 30-60 minutes
- **Weekly**: Finish 1 book every 2 weeks
- **Monthly**: 2 books minimum
- **Yearly**: 24+ books

### Reading Locations:
- Morning: Before work starts
- Commute: Audiobook version
- Evening: Wind down with reading
- Weekend: Longer reading sessions

### Accountability:
- [ ] Share reading list with co-founder
- [ ] Discuss books together
- [ ] Quiz each other on concepts
- [ ] Track books completed
