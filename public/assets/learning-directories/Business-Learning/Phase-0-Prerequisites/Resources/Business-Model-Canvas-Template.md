# Business Model Canvas for CoodLoom

## What is the Business Model Canvas?

The Business Model Canvas is a strategic management template for developing new or documenting existing business models. It's a visual chart with elements describing a firm's value proposition, infrastructure, customers, and finances.

## How to Use This Template

1. Start with **Customer Segments** and **Value Propositions** (right side)
2. Then work on **Channels** and **Customer Relationships**
3. Move to **Revenue Streams**
4. Finally, fill the left side: **Key Resources**, **Key Activities**, **Key Partners**
5. End with **Cost Structure**

**Review and iterate** - This is not a one-time exercise!

## Tips for Completing This Canvas

1. **Be Specific**: Avoid vague descriptions
2. **Use Data**: Include numbers wherever possible
3. **Stay Realistic**: Based on what you know today
4. **Iterate**: This will change as you learn
5. **Collaborate**: Complete with your co-founder
6. **Test Assumptions**: Don't guess, validate with customers
7. **Focus**: It's okay to have a simple model initially

## Next Steps

1. **Complete this canvas** with your co-founder
2. **Identify top 3 assumptions** that need validation
3. **Talk to 10 potential customers** to test assumptions
4. **Iterate based on learnings**
5. **Use this to guide** your business planning and strategy
