## How to Use These Resources

### Week-by-Week Guide

**Week 1: Assessment**
- Complete Scaling-Readiness-Checklist.md
- Review SaaS-Metrics-Reference.md
- Set up Metrics-Dashboard-Template.md
- Identify top 3 processes to document

**Week 2: Documentation**
- Use SOP-Template.md to document processes
- Complete Process-Documentation-Worksheet.md
- Create Customer-Onboarding-Checklist.md
- Set up Support-SLA-Template.md

**Week 3: Automation**
- Review Email-Sequence-Templates.md
- Set up marketing automation
- Create Marketing-Calendar-Template.md
- Automate what you can

**Week 4: Planning**
- Complete Team-Structure-Planner.md
- Review Job-Description-Template.md
- Use Sales-Playbook-Template.md
- Plan your first hire

### By Current Stage

**Solo Founder ($0-30k MRR)**:
- Focus on: Metrics, processes, automation
- Use: SOP-Template, Metrics-Dashboard, Email-Sequences
- Prepare for: First hire

**Small Team ($30-100k MRR)**:
- Focus on: Hiring, sales, support
- Use: Job-Description, Sales-Playbook, Support-SLA
- Prepare for: Team expansion

**Growing Company ($100k+ MRR)**:
- Focus on: Team structure, advanced metrics, systems
- Use: Team-Structure-Planner, all templates
- Prepare for: Rapid scaling

## Getting Help

**When you're stuck**:
1. Review the relevant chapter in Phase 6
2. Check if there's a template for it
3. Search for examples online
4. Ask in entrepreneur communities
5. Consider hiring a consultant/advisor

**When to hire help**:
- **Fractional CMO**: Marketing strategy ($100-300/hr)
- **Sales Consultant**: Sales process ($150-300/hr)
- **Operations Consultant**: Systems & process ($150-250/hr)
- **Recruiting Firm**: First key hires (15-25% of salary)

## Metrics & Progress Tracking

**Monthly Review Questions**:
1. Did we hit our MRR target?
2. Is churn trending down?
3. Are processes documented?
4. Is the team growing appropriately?
5. Are we spending efficiently?

**Quarterly Review Questions**:
1. Are we on track for annual goals?
2. What's working in go-to-market?
3. What needs to change?
4. What should we stop doing?
5. What should we start doing?
