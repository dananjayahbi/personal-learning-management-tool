# Wealth Management Worksheet

## Overview

This worksheet helps you track, protect, and grow your entrepreneurial wealth from pre-exit through post-exit and beyond. Entrepreneurial wealth is different from W-2 wealth—it's concentrated, illiquid, and requires specialized strategies.

**Review Frequency**: Annually (minimum), or after major events (fundraising, secondary sale, exit)

### D. Tax-Efficient Investing

**Strategies to Minimize Taxes on Investments:**

- [ ] **Asset Location**: Put tax-inefficient assets (bonds, REITs) in tax-advantaged accounts, tax-efficient assets (stocks, index funds) in taxable accounts
- [ ] **Tax Loss Harvesting**: Sell losing investments to offset gains
- [ ] **Qualified Dividends**: Hold stocks >60 days for 15-20% tax rate (vs. ordinary income)
- [ ] **Long-Term Capital Gains**: Hold investments >1 year for 15-20% rate (vs. short-term 37%+)
- [ ] **Muni Bonds**: Tax-free income for high earners
- [ ] **Opportunity Zones**: Invest capital gains in OZ fund, defer/reduce tax
- [ ] **Charitable Giving**: Donate appreciated stock (avoid capital gains, get deduction)
- [ ] **Roth Conversions**: Convert traditional IRA to Roth in low-income years
- [ ] **Backdoor Roth**: Contribute to Roth IRA via non-deductible IRA conversion

**Action**: Review tax efficiency with CPA annually.

### B. Identity & Fraud Protection

**Protect Yourself from Identity Theft & Fraud:**

- [ ] **Credit Freeze**: Freeze credit at all 3 bureaus (Equifax, Experian, TransUnion)
- [ ] **Fraud Alerts**: Set fraud alerts if needed
- [ ] **Identity Monitoring**: Service like LifeLock, IdentityGuard ($10-30/month)
- [ ] **Strong Passwords**: Unique passwords for all accounts, use password manager (1Password, LastPass)
- [ ] **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable 2FA on all financial accounts
- [ ] **Secure Email**: Use encrypted email or separate email for financial accounts
- [ ] **Shred Documents**: Shred financial documents before discarding
- [ ] **Monitor Accounts**: Check bank/investment accounts weekly for suspicious activity

### C. Cybersecurity

**Protect Your Digital Wealth:**

- [ ] **Password Manager**: Use 1Password, LastPass, Bitwarden
- [ ] **Two-Factor Authentication**: Enable on all accounts
- [ ] **VPN**: Use VPN on public Wi-Fi
- [ ] **Encrypted Backups**: Backup important documents encrypted (1Password, Dropbox, etc.)
- [ ] **Separate Email**: Use separate email for financial accounts (not public)
- [ ] **Beware Phishing**: Don't click links in emails, always go directly to website
- [ ] **Update Software**: Keep computer and phone OS updated
