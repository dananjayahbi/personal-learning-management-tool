# Exit Preparation Checklist

## Overview

This comprehensive checklist helps you prepare for an eventual exit‚Äîwhether through IPO, acquisition, or positioning for a lifestyle/generational hold. Even if you're years away from exit, many of these items should be addressed early to maximize optionality and value.

**Use this checklist starting in Year 3-5, review annually, and complete fully 18-24 months before planned exit.**

---

## Understanding Your Exit Options

### Three Primary Exit Paths

**Path 1: IPO (Initial Public Offering)**
- **Timeline**: 10-15 years from founding, 2-3 years of prep
- **Requirements**: $100M+ revenue, strong growth, public market appetite
- **Outcome**: Partial liquidity, ongoing public company responsibilities
- **Best for**: Massive markets, sustainable competitive advantages, professional management

**Path 2: Acquisition**
- **Timeline**: 5-10 years from founding, 1-2 years of prep
- **Requirements**: Strategic or financial value to acquirer, clean business
- **Outcome**: Full or partial liquidity, potential earnout
- **Best for**: Strategic value, growing markets, attractive to acquirers

**Path 3: Long-term Hold (No Exit)**
- **Timeline**: Indefinite
- **Requirements**: Sustainable profitability, professional management
- **Outcome**: Ongoing ownership, dividends, generational wealth
- **Best for**: Lifestyle businesses, family businesses, sustainable cash flow

## Conclusion

Exit preparation takes time‚Äîtypically 18-24 months of focused effort. Don't wait until you have an offer or decide to go public to start preparing.

**Key Takeaways:**

1. **Start Early**: Begin preparation 2-3 years before target exit
2. **Be Thorough**: Address all gaps, don't skip "small" issues
3. **Get Help**: Engage professional advisors early
4. **Stay Focused**: Don't let exit prep distract from running the business
5. **Be Patient**: Good exits take time, bad exits are rushed
6. **Maintain Optionality**: Being ready doesn't mean you must exit
