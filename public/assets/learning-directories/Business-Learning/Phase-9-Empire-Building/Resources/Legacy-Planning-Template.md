# Legacy Planning Template

## Overview

This template helps you define and create your legacy‚Äîthe lasting impact that extends beyond your lifetime. Legacy is not just about wealth; it's about the values you lived, the impact you made, and what you'll be remembered for.

**"Legacy is planting seeds in a garden you never get to see."** - Hamilton: An American Musical

---

## Understanding Legacy

### What is Legacy?

Legacy is not:
- ‚ùå Your net worth
- ‚ùå Your job title
- ‚ùå Your achievements list
- ‚ùå How much you're talked about

Legacy is:
- ‚úÖ The positive difference you made in others' lives
- ‚úÖ The values and principles you modeled
- ‚úÖ The institutions and systems you improved
- ‚úÖ The people you inspired and mentored
- ‚úÖ The problems you solved
- ‚úÖ The love and kindness you shared

### Three Dimensions of Legacy

**1. Business Legacy**: What you built and created
**2. Impact Legacy**: The problems you solved and lives you improved
**3. Personal Legacy**: The relationships, values, and family you nurtured

**This template addresses all three dimensions.**
