# Empire Building Checklist

Use this comprehensive checklist to track your progress through the empire building journey.

## Year 1-2: Foundation Phase

### Business Fundamentals
- [ ] CoodLoom POS MVP launched
- [ ] First 10 paying customers acquired
- [ ] Product-market fit validated (NPS >30)
- [ ] $10K+ MRR achieved
- [ ] Basic team assembled (2-5 people)
- [ ] Legal entity formed (LLC or C-Corp)
- [ ] 83(b) election filed (if applicable)
- [ ] Basic accounting system implemented
- [ ] Bank accounts opened and organized

### Financial Milestones
- [ ] Break-even or path to profitability clear
- [ ] Unit economics positive (LTV > 3x CAC)
- [ ] 6 months operating cash reserve
- [ ] Financial model built and maintained
- [ ] Monthly financial review process

### Personal Setup
- [ ] Personal emergency fund (6 months expenses)
- [ ] Basic insurance (health, life, disability)
- [ ] CPA/accountant hired
- [ ] Business attorney identified
- [ ] Personal financial tracking system

## Year 2-3: Growth Phase

### Business Growth
- [ ] 50-100 customers acquired
- [ ] $50K+ MRR achieved
- [ ] Team grown to 8-12 people
- [ ] Repeatable sales process established
- [ ] Customer success program implemented
- [ ] <5% monthly churn rate
- [ ] Positive cash flow achieved

### Brand & Visibility
- [ ] Company blog launched
- [ ] Social media presence established
- [ ] First customer case studies published
- [ ] Local speaking engagements (2-3)
- [ ] Press coverage (local media)

### Planning
- [ ] 3-year strategic plan created
- [ ] Adjacent business opportunities identified
- [ ] Potential revenue streams mapped
- [ ] Industry partnerships explored

## Year 3-5: Diversification Phase

### Second Business Line
- [ ] CoodLoom Software Services launched
- [ ] First 5-10 services clients
- [ ] Services team built (3-5 people)
- [ ] $300K-500K services revenue
- [ ] Separate P&L for each division

### Financial Maturity
- [ ] $2-5M total revenue across divisions
- [ ] Profitable overall
- [ ] Clean financial statements
- [ ] Annual audit completed (if needed)
- [ ] Banking relationships established

### Wealth Building
- [ ] Net worth >$1M (including equity)
- [ ] Diversification started (some liquid assets)
- [ ] Estate plan basics (will, trust)
- [ ] Wealth advisor engaged
- [ ] Tax optimization strategy implemented

### Industry Presence
- [ ] Regular content published (weekly blog)
- [ ] Regional conference speaking (3-5)
- [ ] Industry publication articles (2-3)
- [ ] Strategic partnerships established (2-3)
- [ ] Community program launched

## Year 5-7: Portfolio Building Phase

### Product Expansion
- [ ] Product #2 launched and gaining traction
- [ ] Product #3 in development or launched
- [ ] Cross-sell/upsell programs working
- [ ] Product suite bundles offered
- [ ] 40%+ customers use multiple products

### Scale Operations
- [ ] 40-60 person team
- [ ] Professional management layer
- [ ] Documented processes and systems
- [ ] Department leads hired
- [ ] Culture and values codified

### Financial Performance
- [ ] $8-15M total revenue
- [ ] 25%+ EBITDA margin
- [ ] Strong balance sheet
- [ ] Strategic reserves built
- [ ] Ready to invest in growth

### Industry Leadership
- [ ] National conference speaking
- [ ] Major industry awards won
- [ ] Strategic partnerships with giants
- [ ] Recognized as top 5 in niche
- [ ] Annual user conference launched (optional)

### Personal Wealth
- [ ] Net worth $5-15M
- [ ] Some secondary liquidity achieved (optional)
- [ ] Comprehensive estate plan
- [ ] Tax optimization advanced
- [ ] Philanthropic giving started

## Year 7-10: Empire Phase

### Holding Company Structure
- [ ] Decision made: Create holding company or not
- [ ] If yes: Holding company formed
- [ ] If yes: Subsidiaries reorganized
- [ ] If yes: Inter-company agreements in place
- [ ] If yes: Separate management for each division

### Business Maturity
- [ ] 4-5+ products/divisions operational
- [ ] 100-150+ person team
- [ ] $20-50M+ revenue
- [ ] Market leadership position (top 3)
- [ ] Platform/API layer built

### Innovation & Growth
- [ ] R&D division established
- [ ] Innovation pipeline robust
- [ ] 1-2 acquisitions evaluated or completed
- [ ] Investment arm considered (CoodLoom Ventures)
- [ ] International expansion explored

### Industry Dominance
- [ ] Category-defining position
- [ ] Thought leadership established
- [ ] Industry standards influenced
- [ ] Major brand recognition (60%+ awareness)
- [ ] Ecosystem of partners and integrations

### Exit Planning
- [ ] Exit strategy options evaluated
- [ ] Company prepared for potential exit
- [ ] Valuation and M&A advisor engaged (if considering exit)
- [ ] IPO readiness assessed (if considering)
- [ ] OR: Long-term hold strategy confirmed

### Wealth Management
- [ ] Net worth $20-100M+
- [ ] Comprehensive wealth management
- [ ] Estate plan complete and sophisticated
- [ ] Philanthropic strategy active
- [ ] Family wealth education started

## Year 10+: Legacy Phase

### Business Legacy
- [ ] Company can operate without founder daily involvement
- [ ] Leadership pipeline strong
- [ ] Successor identified (if stepping back)
- [ ] Company positioned for 20+ year horizon
- [ ] Industry leadership sustained

### Financial Legacy
- [ ] Exit completed or sustainable dividends flowing
- [ ] Wealth preserved and growing
- [ ] Multi-generational wealth plan
- [ ] Philanthropic commitments fulfilled
- [ ] Family financially secure

### Impact Legacy
- [ ] 500+ jobs created (direct and indirect)
- [ ] 1,000+ customers served successfully
- [ ] Industry advanced materially
- [ ] $1M+ given to causes
- [ ] 50+ entrepreneurs mentored

### Personal Legacy
- [ ] Book published or major content created
- [ ] Teaching and mentorship systematic
- [ ] Values lived consistently
- [ ] Family relationships strong
- [ ] Life well-lived assessment: Positive

- [ ] Wealth review annual

- [ ] Mentor 2-5 people consistently
- [ ] Board service or advisory roles

### Personal Health
- [ ] Regular exercise routine
- [ ] Annual health checkup
- [ ] Stress management practices
- [ ] Work-life balance maintained

- [ ] Values alignment checked regularly
- [ ] Important moments not missed
- [ ] Present and engaged with loved ones
