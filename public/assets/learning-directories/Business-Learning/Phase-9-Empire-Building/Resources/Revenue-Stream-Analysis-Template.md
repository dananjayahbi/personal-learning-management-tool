## Part 8: CoodLoom Example

### Example: CoodLoom Inventory Management (Year 3)

**Revenue Stream Name**: CoodLoom Inventory
**Launch Date**: Q1 Year 3
**Business Unit**: CoodLoom Software (existing)
**Revenue Type**: Recurring (SaaS add-on)

**Description**: Add-on module to CoodLoom POS that provides advanced inventory management, multi-location tracking, reorder automation, and supplier management for retail/restaurant customers.

**Strategic Alignment**:
- Mission alignment: 5/5 (core product expansion)
- Customer overlap: 5/5 (same customers as POS)
- Technology leverage: 5/5 (same platform and infrastructure)
- Average: 5/5 - **Excellent fit**

**Market Opportunity**:
- TAM: $2B (inventory management software market)
- SAM: $200M (our customer segment: SMB retail/restaurants)
- SOM: $20M (Year 5 target: 10% of SAM)

**Pricing**: $49/month add-on to CoodLoom POS (base: $99/month)

**Unit Economics**:
- CAC: $300 (same as POS, can upsell to existing customers)
- LTV: $2,352 (49 × 48 months avg lifespan)
- LTV:CAC = 7.8:1 **Excellent**
- Gross margin: 85% (software)

**5-Year Revenue Forecast**:
- Year 1: $150K (30% of existing POS customers adopt, ~250 customers)
- Year 2: $600K (grow to 1,000 customers)
- Year 3: $1.5M (2,500 customers)
- Year 4: $3M (5,000 customers)
- Year 5: $5M (8,500 customers)

**Investment Required**: $250K
- Development: $150K (6 months, 2 engineers)
- Marketing: $50K (materials, launch)
- Sales: $50K (training, collateral)

**Portfolio Fit**:
- Before: POS 90%, Services 10%
- After Year 5: POS 60%, Inventory 25%, Services 15% - **Better balance**

**Strategic Value**:
- Customer retention: 5/5 (harder to switch with 2 products)
- Competitive moat: 4/5 (integrated solution beats point solutions)
- Market expansion: 3/5 (same customers, but deeper penetration)
- Average: 4/5 - **High strategic value**

**Total Score**: 4.5 / 5.0 - **Excellent - STRONG GO**

**Decision**: ✅ **GO** - Launch in Q1 Year 3
