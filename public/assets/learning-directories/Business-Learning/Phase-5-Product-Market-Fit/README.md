# Phase 5: Product-Market Fit

## Overview
Transform your POS system from "something we built" to "something customers desperately need." This phase focuses on validation, iteration, and achieving true product-market fit.

## Chapters
1. **Understanding Product-Market Fit** - What it is and how to know you have it
2. **Customer Development** - Systematic customer discovery and validation
3. **Feature Prioritization** - Building what matters most
4. **Pricing Validation** - Testing if customers will pay your price
5. **Customer Feedback Loops** - Collecting and acting on feedback
6. **Iteration Process** - How to improve based on learnings
7. **Retention Metrics** - Are customers staying or churning?
8. **Expansion Revenue** - Upselling and cross-selling strategies

## Key Outcomes
- ✓ Validated product-market fit
- ✓ Low customer churn
- ✓ Clear feature roadmap
- ✓ Repeatable customer success

## Time Estimate
3-6 months of continuous validation and iteration
