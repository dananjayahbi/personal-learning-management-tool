# Phase 7: Leadership & Management

## Overview
Transform from founder to CEO. This phase develops your leadership skills to manage teams, make strategic decisions, and build company culture.

## Chapters
1. **Leadership Fundamentals** - Being an effective CEO
2. **Team Management** - Leading and motivating your team
3. **Decision Making** - Strategic thinking and prioritization
4. **Communication Skills** - Clear, effective communication
5. **Company Culture** - Building the CoodLoom culture
6. **Conflict Resolution** - Handling difficult situations
7. **Time Management** - CEO priorities and delegation
8. **Board Management** - Working with advisors and investors
9. **Vision Setting** - Long-term strategic planning

## Key Outcomes
- ✓ Strong leadership skills
- ✓ Effective team management
- ✓ Clear company culture
- ✓ Strategic decision-making ability

## Time Estimate
Ongoing development - leadership is a lifelong journey
