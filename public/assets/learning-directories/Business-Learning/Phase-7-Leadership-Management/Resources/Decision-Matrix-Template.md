## Template 2: Type 1 vs Type 2 Decision Framework

**For**: Determining how much time to spend on a decision

### Type Classification

**Ask: Is this decision reversible?**

- [ ] **Type 2 (Reversible)**: Can change it later without major consequences
  - **Time to decide**: Minutes to hours
  - **Process**: Decide with information you have, iterate if wrong
  - **Who decides**: Whoever is closest to the problem

- [ ] **Type 1 (Irreversible)**: Can't easily undo, major consequences
  - **Time to decide**: Days to weeks
  - **Process**: Deep analysis, multiple perspectives, careful consideration

## How to Use These Templates

### For Small Decisions (< $10k impact)
- Use Template 1 (Simple Matrix) or skip frameworks entirely
- Decide quickly
- Learn from outcomes

### For Medium Decisions ($10k-100k impact)
- Use Template 1 (Simple Matrix) or Template 3 (Strategic Canvas)
- Take a few days
- Involve 2-3 stakeholders

### For Large Decisions (> $100k impact)
- Use Templates 3, 4, 6 (Strategic Canvas, RAPID, Pre-Mortem)
- Take 1-2 weeks
- Involve full leadership team

### For Life/Career Decisions
- Use Template 5 (Regret Minimization)
- Take as long as needed
- Consult mentors and advisors

---

## Remember

**Templates are tools, not substitutes for judgment**
- Use when they help
- Skip when they slow you down
- Adapt to your needs

**The goal is better decisions, not perfect process**

**Track your decisions to improve over time**
