## 2. Market Size & Growth

### Understanding TAM, SAM, and SOM

**TAM (Total Addressable Market)**: If you could sell to every potential customer
**SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)**: Customers you can realistically reach
**SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)**: Customers you'll actually capture

### Calculating CoodLoom's Market Size

#### TAM: Total Addressable Market

**All U.S. Food Service Establishments**:
- Total restaurants: 1,000,000
- Average POS spend: $2,400/year (software + hardware)
- **TAM = 1,000,000 √ó $2,400 = $2.4 billion**

#### SAM: Serviceable Addressable Market

**CoodLoom's Focus**: Quick-service restaurants and coffee shops with 1-3 locations

**Market Segmentation**:
- Quick-service restaurants: 400,000
- Coffee shops: 37,000
- **Subtotal**: 437,000 establishments

**Revenue Range Filter**: $500K-$2M annual revenue
- Estimated 45% of establishments fit this range
- **Filtered count**: 437,000 √ó 0.45 = ~197,000

**Location Filter**: 1-3 locations
- Estimated 78% are single or small multi-location
- **Final count**: 197,000 √ó 0.78 = ~154,000 establishments

**CoodLoom SAM**:
- Target customers: 154,000
- Average annual revenue per customer: $2,388 ($199/mo √ó 12 months)
- **SAM = 154,000 √ó $2,388 = $367 million**

#### SOM: Serviceable Obtainable Market

**Realistic Market Capture** (5-year projection):

**Year 1**: 0.03% of SAM

**Year 3**: 0.3% of SAM

- $1.2 million annual revenue

**Year 5**: 1.3% of SAM

**10-Year Aspiration**: 5% of SAM

**Why This is Realistic**:

**Projected Growth** (2024-2029):

## 6. Buying Behavior & Decision Process

**For Small Operations (1-2 locations)**:
- **Decision Maker**: Owner
- **Influencers**: None (sole decision)
- **Users**: Owner + staff

**For Slightly Larger (3-5 locations)**:
- **Decision Maker**: Owner or GM
- **Influencers**: Store managers, head chef
- **Users**: All front-of-house staff

### Buying Decision Timeline

**Stage 1: Awareness** (Weeks 1-2)
- Triggered by: Frustration with current system, competitor recommendation, online search
- Actions: Google search, ask peers, read reviews
- CoodLoom Strategy: SEO, G2/Capterra reviews, content marketing

**Stage 2: Consideration** (Weeks 3-6)
- Actions: Research 3-5 alternatives, request demos, check pricing
- Concerns: Switching cost, learning curve, reliability
- CoodLoom Strategy: Easy demo signup, clear migration plan, case studies

**Stage 3: Evaluation** (Weeks 7-10)
- Actions: Live demo, trial if available, reference calls

- Actions: Final negotiation, contract signing, implementation planning
