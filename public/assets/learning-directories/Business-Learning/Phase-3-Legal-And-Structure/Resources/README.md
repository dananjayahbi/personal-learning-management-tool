## Cost-Saving Tips

**DIY Safely**:
- Use templates for internal planning
- Standard NDAs (low risk)
- Basic privacy policy (using template + generator)

**Hire Lawyer For**:
- Founders agreements (high stakes)
- Incorporation (one-time cost)
- Customer agreements (if large deals)
- Employment disputes
- Trademark filing (worth the investment)

**Middle Ground**:
- Start with template
- Customize for your situation
- Pay lawyer for 1-hour review ($300-500)
- Much cheaper than drafting from scratch

## Professional Help

**When You Need a Lawyer**:
- Raising capital (securities law)
- Significant customer contracts ($50K+)
- Employee disputes or terminations
- Trademark opposition or infringement
- Complex contract negotiations
- Regulatory investigations

**Finding a Lawyer**:
- **Startup lawyers**: Understand tech/SaaS
- **Referrals**: Ask other founders
- **Directories**: Martindale, Avvo
- **Incubators**: Often have legal partners
- **Cost**: $300-600/hour, or flat fees for specific services

**Legal Insurance**:
- LegalZoom: $20-40/month (basic)
- Rocket Lawyer: $40/month
- Covers: Document review, legal questions, discounts on services
