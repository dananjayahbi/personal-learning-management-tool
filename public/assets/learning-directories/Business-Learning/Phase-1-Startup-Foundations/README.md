# Phase 1: Startup Foundations

## Overview
Now that you understand business basics, this phase focuses on laying the groundwork for CoodLoom. You'll learn how to validate your idea, build your team structure, and prepare for launch.

## What You'll Learn
- Validating your POS system with existing customers
- Partnership and equity structuring (critical for you and your co-founder)
- Early-stage team building
- Minimum Viable Product (MVP) strategy
- Go-to-market planning

## Chapters in This Phase

### Chapter 1: Idea Validation & Customer Discovery
Learn how to systematically validate that your POS system solves real problems.

### Chapter 2: Co-Founder Dynamics & Equity Split
**CRITICAL FOR YOU**: How to structure partnership, split equity fairly, and protect your CEO position.

### Chapter 3: Building Your MVP
Turning your existing POS system into a market-ready product.

### Chapter 4: Initial Market Strategy
How to leverage your existing customers and acquire more.

### Chapter 5: Branding & Positioning
Creating the CoodLoom brand identity and domain strategy.

## Time Estimate
4-6 weeks to complete and implement

## Key Outcomes
By the end of this phase, you should have:
- ✓ Clear equity agreement with your co-founder
- ✓ Validated product-market fit
- ✓ Initial customer pipeline
- ✓ CoodLoom brand established
- ✓ Ready to formally launch
