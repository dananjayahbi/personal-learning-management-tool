## Interview Script

### Opening (2 minutes)

```
"Hi [Name], thanks so much for taking the time to chat. I really appreciate it.

Just to set expectations, I'm doing research to understand the challenges 
that [industry] businesses face with their POS systems. I'm NOT trying to 
sell you anything today - I genuinely just want to learn from your experience.

This should take about 20-30 minutes. I'll ask you some questions about 
how you currently handle transactions and what works or doesn't work for you.

Does that sound good? Great! And just to confirm, I can take notes during 
our conversation, right?"
```

## Next Steps After Interview

**Within 2 Hours**:
1. Fill out all post-interview notes
2. Update your CRM/tracking spreadsheet
3. Send thank you email

**Within 24 Hours**:
4. Analyze for patterns (if 5+ interviews done)
5. Update your ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**Within 1 Week**:
7. Follow up with any promised information
8. Request introductions to referrals
