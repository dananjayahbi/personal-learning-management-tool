## Startup Budget Breakdown

### Minimum Monthly Budget (Pre-Revenue)

**Essential** (Can't launch without):
- Hosting (DigitalOcean): $25/mo
- Domain: $1/mo (annual / 12)
- Email (Google Workspace): $12/mo
- SSL (via Cloudflare): Free
- **Total Essential**: ~$40/mo

**Recommended** (Should have):
- Design (Figma): $12/mo
- Error tracking (Sentry): Free
- Analytics: Free
- Uptime monitoring: Free
- **Total with Recommended**: ~$50/mo

**Nice to Have** (When revenue starts):
- CRM (HubSpot): Free ‚Üí $50/mo
- Customer support (Freshdesk): Free ‚Üí $15/agent
- Marketing (Mailchimp): Free ‚Üí $20/mo
- **Total with Nice to Have**: ~$135/mo

- **Total with Advanced**: ~$335/mo

## Tool Selection Principles

### Choose Tools That:
‚úì **Have free tiers or trials** (test before committing)
‚úì **Scale with you** (free ‚Üí paid as you grow)
‚úì **Integrate well** (play nice with other tools)
‚úì **Have good documentation** (easy to learn)
‚úì **Are industry standard** (easy to hire for)
‚úì **Have active communities** (get help easily)

### Avoid Tools That:
‚úó Require upfront annual payment (before validation)
‚úó Have terrible reviews (check G2, Capterra)
‚úó Lock you in (hard to migrate data)
‚úó Are overkill for your stage (enterprise tools)
‚úó Have poor support (when you're the only dev)

## Recommended Stack for CoodLoom

### MVP Stack (Keep it simple):

**Frontend**: React/Next.js
**Backend**: Node.js (Express) or Python (FastAPI)
**Database**: PostgreSQL
**Hosting**: DigitalOcean or Railway
**Payments**: Stripe
**Email**: SendGrid
**Monitoring**: Sentry + UptimeRobot
**Analytics**: Google Analytics + Mixpanel

**Total cost**: $50-100/month

### When to Upgrade

**At 10 customers**:
- Add: CRM (HubSpot paid)
- Add: Customer support (Freshdesk)
- Cost: +$80/month

**At 50 customers**:
- Upgrade: More hosting resources
- Add: APM (New Relic/Datadog)
- Add: Chat support
- Cost: +$200/month

**At 100 customers**:
- Everything above +
- Consider: Dedicated servers
- Consider: Advanced security
- Cost: $500-1,000/month
