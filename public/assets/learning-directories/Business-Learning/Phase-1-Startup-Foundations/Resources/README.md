# Phase 1 Resources

## Overview

This folder contains templates, checklists, and reference materials to support your Phase 1 journey with CoodLoom.

## Contents

### Templates
1. **Customer-Interview-Template.md** - Complete interview guide for customer discovery
2. **Founders-Agreement-Checklist.md** - Key points for your co-founder agreement
3. **MVP-Launch-Checklist.md** - Step-by-step launch preparation
4. **Sales-Email-Templates.md** - Proven email templates for outreach
5. **Brand-Strategy-Worksheet.md** - Framework for defining your brand

### Reference Materials
6. **Validation-Scorecard.md** - Assess if your idea is validated
7. **Pricing-Strategy-Guide.md** - How to price CoodLoom
8. **Competitor-Analysis-Template.md** - Analyze your competition
9. **Recommended-Tools.md** - Software tools for startups

### Quick Links
10. **Useful-Links.md** - External resources, books, courses

## How to Use These Resources

1. **Start with Customer-Interview-Template.md** as you validate your idea
2. **Use Founders-Agreement-Checklist.md** when structuring your partnership
3. **Follow MVP-Launch-Checklist.md** as you build and launch
4. **Leverage Sales-Email-Templates.md** for customer acquisition
5. **Complete Brand-Strategy-Worksheet.md** to define your brand

All templates are meant to be customized for CoodLoom. Copy, edit, and make them your own!
